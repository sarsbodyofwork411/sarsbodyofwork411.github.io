Research

Our research focuses on reliable, adaptive, and uncertainty-aware machine learning.

Research Themes

01

Bayesian & Uncertainty-Aware Learning

We study learning algorithms that quantify predictive uncertainty and improve reliability under distribution shift.

02

Continual and Adaptive Learning

We develop AI systems that can adapt to new tasks, environments, and data streams without forgetting previous knowledge.

03

Multimodal Foundation Models

We are interested in learning from multiple modalities such as vision, language, sensors, and structured data.

04

AI for Scientific and Physical Systems

We explore machine learning methods for scientific computing, physical systems, and real-world decision-making problems.
